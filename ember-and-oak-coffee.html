Our Story

Built one cup, one regular, at a time.

2016
A single window counter
We started with one espresso machine and four bar stools, roasting in small batches so nothing sat around long enough to go stale.
2019
The roastery expands
Demand outgrew the back room, so we moved roasting into its own space and started sourcing direct from three family farms.
2023
This room
We built the space you're browsing right now — designed for lingering, with the machine still in plain view.
01 · MISSION

Slow the ritual down

Every cup is made to order, never batch-brewed and held. If there's a line, that's the point — good coffee takes a minute.

02 · SOURCING

Direct trade, named farms

We know the farmers behind our beans by name, and we pay above market rate every harvest.

03 · SPACE

A room to actually sit in

No turnover targets. Bring a book, take a call, stay for the afternoon.
